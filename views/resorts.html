Resort Amenities

Experience the best of the world with our resorts

Calling All Adventure-Seekers

We’re a Jackson Hole luxury hotel in the heart of Teton Village and only steps from some of the best skiing and snowboarding in North America. Relax in our rooftop spa, enjoy a cocktail before dining in one of our two restaurants, soak in our infinity pool. You really can have it all.

Unexpected and Unpretentious

From our mountain boutique’s spirited interiors, laced with just the right amount of authenticity and attitude, to the luxurious amenities curated for those gearing up to ‘go big,’ our goal is to fuel inspiration for life on the edge in a space that you can make your home base.

Dine With Us

Fuel your day of exploration with breakfast, lunch or dinner at our locally-beloved onsite restaurant, Bar Enoteca. For fine Italian cuisine and a post-slopes après, head into our Il Villaggio Osteria, which also services our mouth-watering in-room dining.

Ski Valet & On-Site Rentals

Enjoy the convenience of on-site ski rentals at Ski Valet. Our Ski Valet retrieves, collects and stores your skis, offers tips and advice on the best runs and conditions, and has trail maps and lift status information.

Hotel Terra Occupancy Fee

An 8% Occupancy Fee of your nightly room rate (plus applicable taxes) will be applied to all reservations. This fee is in addition to the room rate per night. This fee is intended to support the community’s infrastructure, public transportation subsidies, additional flight capacity into Jackson Hole, as well as other costs associated with our destination’s seasonal operations.
